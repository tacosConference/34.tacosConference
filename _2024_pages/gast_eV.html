About g.a.s.t.
The Society for Academic Study Preparation and Test Development (g.a.s.t.) is a non-profit organisation that

  • paves the way for international students to successful studies in Germany through standardised language and aptitude tests,
  • supports German universities by providing fair and valid selection tools for their admissions procedures,
  • prepares international students for the start of their studies with the Deutsch-Uni Online (DUO) learning platform, both linguistically and culturally, and
  • conducts research as a university-affiliated institute on issues related to testing linguistic and cognitive skills.
