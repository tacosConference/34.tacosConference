Participants

In May 2025, sponsors will have a special opportunity to be represented at a large student conference in the field of computational linguistics. The TaCoS is attended by up to a 100 of German and international students each year.

With the recent release of ChatGPT and the growing awareness of neural networks and AI, people around the world are more than ever interested in the contribution of computational linguistics to our society. The students who attend this conference are studying and working with some of the pioneering technology of our time, and TaCoS is a unique opportunity for sponsors to support and connect with this versatile group of academics.

  

What does my donation go toward?

  • Comfortable accomodations for traveling students
  • Food and drinks for the participants over the three days of the conference
  • Diversity scholarship to help students in need by waiving participant fees and aiding with accessibility
  • Making the conference as beneficial and enjoyable as possible for all participants

About TaCoS

The Tagung der Computerlinguistik-Studierenden (TaCoS) was first organized in 1992 and takes place once a year. It offers students of computational linguistics and adjacent fields a chance to exchange views during talks and workshops.

The conference is hosted by teams of student volunteers at their university. This year's host is Ruhr Universit√§t Bochum. Participants traditionally receive accommodation and breakfast in exchange for a small fee.
  

What's included in the conference schedule?

  • Talks, workshops, and poster sessions - given by students
  • Keynote speeches given by established academics
  • Exploring opportunities during a job fair
  • Connecting through social networking events
