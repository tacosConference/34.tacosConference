Application deadline: July 1st 2024
We currently offer 6 diversity scholarships of 75€ each, sponsored by Digital Humanities and IGDD. The scholarships will be sent out after the conference, directly by our sponsors.

How to apply

Send us an email, introducing yourself and explaining why you think you are eligible for a scholarship. Please refer to the scholarship criteria below to write your introduction text.

Please don't forget to include the following info:

  • Which scholarship you are applying to
  • Who you are, what university you are affiliated with

    • Diversity Scholarship by Digital Humanities

    2 diversity scholarships will be given by Digital Humanities im deutschsprachigen Raum (DHd). The purpose of this scholarship is to support participants who need aid to attend the conference. Even though it is not obligatory to hold a talk, we will prioritize participants who give a talk for this scholarship.

    The following criteria will impact the selection process:

  • Distance between your home university to the TaCoS venue
  • First-time participation
  • No financial support of your university
  • Your abstract’s quality
  • General hardships that may hinder you to attend the conference

    • Diversity Scholarship by IGDD

    4 diversity scholarships will be given by the Internationale Gesellschaft für Dialektologie des Deutschen (IGDD). Only the participants who hold a talk with a focus on language variation (ideally for German) are eligible for this scholarship.

    Make sure you include some sentences about:

  • What your research is about
  • Personally, what is important to you about research into language variation

    • Contact

    If you have any further questions regarding the registration you can send us a mail at tacosconference2025@lists.rub.de.
