Billing and cancellation

Upon receiving your registration form, we will send the billing information to the email you have provided. The participation fee is to be paid until the registration deadline. You will get another email from us after, confirming that the transfer and, thus, your registration were successful. The participation fee can be refunded for cancellations received by us no later than two weeks before the conference.
