Topics of Interest

We welcome abstracts for presentations (20 minutes) and workshops (1 hour), on broadly the following main topics:

  • Computational Linguistics
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Digital Humanities with a linguistics component
  • Computer Science

All work that you find interesting or are proud of can be submitted! Your bachelors or masters thesis, a term-paper, work done as a student assistent or simply a hobby project.

  

Submission instructions

Abstracts may be submitted in English or German and should contain a body of around 300 words excluding title and references and should be submitted as PDF. Abstracts can be submitted via the EasyChair Link below.

Below are some intructions that should be helpful:

  1. Follow the link to get to the Call for papers: EasyChair TaCoS 2024 form
  2. Create an account. Note that the email for your account and the email you use to submit must be the same.
  3. Click on Make a new submission underneath Author.
  4. Read the instructions at the top of the page. Fill out the author(s) information, then the information on the title and abstract of your talk.
  5. Upload the abstract via pdf.
